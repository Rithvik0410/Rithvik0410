## ⚡ About Me

I live at the intersection of three worlds that don't usually share a business card:

- 🎧 **Musician** — guitar & piano, chasing that perfect chord progression at 2 AM
- 🖌️ **Artist** — traditional painting, digital illustration, and 3D work in **Blender**
- 👨‍💻 **Software / Data Engineer** — building pipelines and systems that (hopefully) don't break in production

Currently deep in the **data engineering** rabbit hole — **ADF, Databricks, PySpark, Power BI** — while still finding time to pick up a guitar or open a canvas.

- 🌱 Currently leveling up in: `Azure Data Factory` · `Databricks` · `PySpark` · `Power BI`
- 🎯 Always exploring: efficient systems, new sounds, new mediums
- 📫 Reach me at **rithvikshettymails@gmail.com**
- ⚡ Fun fact: my commit history and my sketchbook are equally messy
